Meatballs

Description

I've been making these meatballs for years. Everyone always raves about them!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds Meatloaf mix (1/3 Beef, 1/3 Veal, 1/3 Pork)
  • 1 teaspoon Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmaggiano-Reggiano Cheese
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 10 leaves of fresh Basil
  • A few springs of fresh Parsley, to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Roll basil leaves together lengthwise (about 3 at a time) and thinly slice them
  3. Chop parsley finely
  4. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, gently mixing with your hands until mixture appears even
  5. Using your hands, roll into 1.5 oz balls (you shold get about 21 meatballs)
  6. Place meatballs onto half sheet pan, leaving plents of room around each one
  7. Roast for 20 minutes or until the internal temperature is 165 degrees
  8. Enjoy!
